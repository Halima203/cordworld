About Us

At CordWorld, we believe that every knot tells a story. Born from a love of texture, tradition, and timeless design, our shop is a celebration of macrame in all its forms—from delicate wall hangings to bold, functional pieces that bring warmth and character to your space. Each item is handcrafted with care, using natural fibers and thoughtful techniques passed down through generations. We’re more than just a shop—we’re a community of makers, dreamers, and lovers of cozy corners. Whether you're decorating a sunlit reading nook, gifting something meaningful, or simply indulging in the beauty of handmade art, CordWorld is here to wrap your world in softness and soul. Welcome to our world of knots, cords, and creativity.
